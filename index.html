About Me:

Hello! Thanks for visiting my page, let me introduce myself. My name is Max and I’m a junior at the University of Pennsylvania. Currently, I am pursuing a double major in Computer Science and History in the College of Arts and Sciences.

Born and raised in rural Ohio, I’ve made the transition from sprawling country roads to the dense urban jungle of Philadelphia. The last two years have been an amazing journey, full of intellectual, professional, and personal development. Now, at the midpoint of my college career, I am inching toward graduation and the next chapter of my life, the professional world. My career interests include Technological Consulting, Web/Software Development, and management within the Tech Sector. In the years to come, I hope to become established in the technology industry, either at a major firm or a fresh startup.

Why tech? Computing, our greatest invention, has the immense potential of changing the world, as it already has. The acceleration and proliferation of automation, Big Data, and machine learning, and its impact on society and the economy, presents a unique challenge for humankind. My goal is to be in the midst of that change, either guiding people through the rapid technological revolution, or helping forge new paths for others to follow.

My personal activities include fitness and sports, reading and writing, and music. I’m a massive soccer fan, and play at least once a week. Even with the hectic schedules of college, I try to make time for personal reading, as well as keeping a nightly journal. With music, I have recently started DJing, which has become an exciting creative outlet.
